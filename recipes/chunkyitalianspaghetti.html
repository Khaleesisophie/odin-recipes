Italian Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic powder, sugar, parsley, salt, oregano, basil, and pepper in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Lower heat to medium-low, cover saucepan, and simmer until flavors blend, about 30 minutes.
